📱 How to Test:

🔧 Troubleshooting:

  • If QR codes don't scan: Make sure your phone has a QR scanner app or use the camera app
  • If URLs don't work: Check that XAMPP is running and the database has items
  • If pages don't load: Check the browser console for errors
